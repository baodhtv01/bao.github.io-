About BGV Academy

BGV Academy is an educational institute for children founded in 2010, an institute under the BGV education system with about 1,000 students enrolled each year.
During more than 10 years of establishment, the institute is growing day by day and is a leader in the field of children's education in Vietnam.
Academy have trained and helped countless children develop to their full potential.
  • With more than 10 years of experience in exploring, researching children's thinking and development. BGV Acdemy wants to bring practical, but effective, training programs to help children discover their own capabilities.
  • With modern equipment and materials, we guarantee to bring children the best development conditions.
  • With a team of experienced and enthusiastic teachers will help children maximize their inherent potential.
With the educational slogan "Do not let children be left behind" we bring the best to children.
